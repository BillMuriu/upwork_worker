You can find the definitions of fields in the appendix below. 1. Clean the dataset. Point out anything you find, that required cleaning. 2. Plot a count of bookings over time. Point out anything you notice about the timeseries. 3. Analyse our activity categories. Tell us anything interesting you find about how they differ in net revenue (NR) and gross merchandise value (GMV). Are there other differences between categories that you notice? 4. We would like you to discover an insight into our business using this data set. You can take your analysis in any direction you like.
